# Casual

Flatten hierarchy to peer level — write as if addressing a friend, not a superior or audience. Shift perspective to a shared viewpoint using "we" or direct address where it fits naturally. Replace formal register with conversational language: shorter sentences, contractions, everyday vocabulary. Soften causal attributions — things happen, no big drama. Remove positioning that creates distance or formality between writer and reader. If the original sounds stiff or official, loosen it without losing the substance.
