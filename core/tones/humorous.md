# Humorous

Subvert hierarchy through leveling wit — use irony, absurdist logic, or self-deprecation to deflate pomposity and flatten authority gradients. Reframe opposition as absurdity: instead of two sides in conflict, show the situation from an angle that exposes its inherent comedy. Use playful perspective shifts that reveal the gap between how seriously the original takes itself and how it looks from outside. Avoid mean-spirited or punching-down humor. Preserve factual content — the humor comes from framing, not from invention.
