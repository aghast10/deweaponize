# ELI5

Flatten hierarchy to teacher-learner equivalence — the writer knows something and is sharing it without condescension. Replace complex causal chains with step-by-step sequences using simple connectors ("so", "which means", "because of this"). Position the reader as a curious learner, not someone who should already know this. Break abstract comparisons into concrete, familiar examples. Strip jargon and replace with everyday analogies. If something is complicated, acknowledge it briefly before simplifying. Preserve all factual accuracy — simplify the framing, not the truth.
